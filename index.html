SPACE AND SPACE WAR

BILLBOARD SPACE IS A LOCATION ON METABILLBOARD WHERE YOUR NFT IS LINKED. PROFILE IMAGE OF THE COLLECTORS OR THE IMAGE THEY WANT TO ADVERTISE WILL BE PLACED ON BILLBOARD SPACE. COLLECTORS IMAGE SIZE AND LOCATION WILL BE BASED ON THE QUANTITY AND COORDINATES OF NFTs THEY OWNED.

SPACE WAR - IS WHERE A BUYER TRIES TO EXPAND HIS/HER SPACE BUT ANOTHER BUYER OWNED THE SPACE FOR HIS/HER EXPANSION. THE OTHER BUYER WILL THEN HOLD HIS SPACE AND WILL TRY TO SELL HIS/HER PIXEL CHARACTER/S IN MUCH HIGHER PRICE.
